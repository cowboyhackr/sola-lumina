Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app require an internet connection?
No. All etymology data is bundled with the app. An internet connection is only used as a fallback for word definitions.

A word I searched has no results. Why?
The etymology database covers over 600,000 words but some rare or very modern terms may not have entries yet. Try searching for the base form of the word.

How do I report incorrect etymology data?
Please email us with the word and what looks wrong. Our data comes from Wiktionary's structured etymology database and we appreciate corrections.
