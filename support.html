Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I link my brokerage accounts?

Currently, syncing with your brokerage account is not supported. You can manually enter your investments for now.

How can I track my total portfolio value?

Your total portfolio value is automatically updated in real-time when the market is open. It’s displayed on the portfolio summary.
