Word Radicals - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Overview

Word Radicals does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is fully respected.

No Account Required

Word Radicals does not require you to create an account or sign in. There is no login, no registration, and no user profiles.

No Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or tracking data of any kind. Your searches stay on your device.

No Third-Party Services

Word Radicals does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Offline by Design

All etymology data is stored locally on your device. The app may connect to the internet only as an optional fallback for word definitions, but no personal data is sent.
